Learn, Contribute, Bring your ideas to life!

We are an open community of passionate learners from different backgrounds and experience levels. We learn from each other regardless of who we are and actively encourage mentorship programs between our peers.


You are not alone in learning, join our Github community and view, learn or contribute to our open source projects and codes! Everyone is welcome to share their ideas, propose a project or even become the lead for it if you want!


There are no hard rules on how projects are conducted or how teams are structured, it's all up to you and your group. To make it happen, you can send us a project proposal through Discord or via our Facebook group and if in case there's a need to make it clear, we have ZERO tolerance for any form of harassment and discrimination.

Ready

Let's begin by learning Git/GitHub along with HTML/CSS so you can begin coding with the group! Of course we will help, so don't be shy or embarassed; feel free to ask questions!

Set

Look for projects and join, it's that simple! You can submit your own proposal, structure the group however the team prefers. Don't hesitate and just join projects you are interested in, you're not alone and you're all set!

Go

Don't stay in the tutorial phase forever, you're all set -- just go! Start coding and immerse yourself in building and sharing and programming BUT don't stop there. Please take the time to teach, and eventually -- lead!

FAQs

how do I reach your group?

you can reach us through our Facebook group or Github dev team page as well as our Discord channel.


how do I join the group?

Join our Facebook page then contact us through chat, everything else is easy from there.


is there a requirement for joining?

Not really. Politeness should be a given and when in doubt, be professional. Other than that, learning HTML/CSS and Git/Github on your own would be great, but even if you don't, feel free to join anyway.


how do I join a project, exactly?

  • First join the Facebook group and Github dev team.
  • View the projects and their details on Github.
  • See a project you like? Head back to the Facebook page and look for a poll.
  • "Vote" for the project you like on the said poll.
  • Admins will send you an invite on Github to join the specific project.

The process needs streamlining but we are working on it and if you want, you can even help us out! :)


how do I submit a proposal?

To submit a proposal, you will have to join the Discord channel and choose the specific #project-proposal sub-channel. Follow the template laid out there and simply submit.


does the group have a preferred type of project?

For now, websites and web apps. Apart from that, it's a free for all. Submit a project proposal and see how many people are interested in working on it.


will I get paid?

No. Long answer, we are all here to learn and practice so everything we work on is open-source. Of course once the project is done you can use the codes however you like and earn from there.


can I ask your group to make something for free?

You can submit a proposal and see if people are interested, though everything we work on is open source so it will be free for everyone and not just for you.


can I commission your group to make me something?

Not as a group but for sure you can join the group and look for talent there.
