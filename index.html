Learn, Contribute, Bring your ideas to life

Learn:

We are an open community of passionate learners from different backgrounds and experiences. We learn from each other regardless of who we are. We encourage mentorship programs between our peers. Almost everyone in the group are self-taught, too. You are not alone. Every projects are open for viewing if you want to see other member's codes. You just have to join our Github team.

Contribute:

Join and help others build their projects. You can contribute by giving feedbacks or just by coding with their team. To join a team, we make polls and announcements on projects that are in need of members.

Bring your ideas to life:

As part of the community, everyone is welcome to share their ideas by implementing their own project. This means they are free to become Project Team Leaders themselves and has every right to turn the project into something they want it to be. They don't have to be Project Team leaders if they wish to and find another one to lead for them or they can have no leaders at all to maximize freedom of speech. Anything is possible in our community.

FAQs

How do I join a project, exactly?

Join the FB group then "vote" for the current project polls. Voting for the project means you want to be a member of that project. For more details on the project, you will have to go to the GitHub page. It's not streamlined yet but we are working on it.

How do I submit a proposal?

Join our discord! We have the channel #project-proposals and fill out this template: Project Name: [What is your app or website called? ] Description: [Describe your project and its features] Team requirements: [What roles do you need for that project and how many members?] Tech Tools to use: [What are the technologies you are going to use for your project?]

I want to help, how?

Voluntary Training Program by Mam Jem, help people get up to speed by leading projects for beginners. Teach Git/GitHub and usually HTML/CSS/JS frontend projects. If you want to help in some other way, please feel free to tell me personally (@paps in FB chat) so we can get you sorted out.
