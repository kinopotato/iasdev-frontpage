Ready:

Let's begin by learning Git/GitHub along with HTML/CSS so you can begin coding with the group! Of course we will help, so don't be shy or embarassed; feel free to ask questions!

Set:

Look for projects and join, it's that simple! You can submit your own proposal, structure the group however the team prefers. Don't hesitate and just join projects you are interested in, you're not alone and you're all set!

Go:

Don't stay in the tutorial phase forever, you're all set -- just go! Start coding and immerse yourself in building and sharing and programming BUT don't stop there. Please take the time to teach, and eventually -- lead!

Learn, Contribute, Bring your ideas to life!

We are an open community of passionate learners from different backgrounds and experience levels. We learn from each other regardless of who we are and actively encourage mentorship programs between our peers. You are not alone in learning, join our Github community and view, learn or contribute to our open source projects and codes! Everyone is welcome to share their ideas, propose a project or even become the lead for it if you want! There are no hard rules on how projects are conducted or how teams are structured, it's all up to you and your group. To make it happen, you can send us a project proposal through Discord or via our Facebook group and if in case there's a need to make it clear, we have ZERO tolerance for any form of harassment and discrimination.

FAQs

How do I join a project, exactly?

Join the FB group then "vote" for the current project polls. Voting for the project means you want to be a member of that project. For more details on the project, you will have to go to the GitHub page. It's not streamlined yet but we are working on it.

How do I submit a proposal?

Join our discord! We have the channel #project-proposals and fill out this template: Project Name: [What is your app or website called? ] Description: [Describe your project and its features] Team requirements: [What roles do you need for that project and how many members?] Tech Tools to use: [What are the technologies you are going to use for your project?]

I want to help, how?

Voluntary Training Program by Mam Jem, help people get up to speed by leading projects for beginners. Teach Git/GitHub and usually HTML/CSS/JS frontend projects. If you want to help in some other way, please feel free to tell me personally (@paps in FB chat) so we can get you sorted out.
